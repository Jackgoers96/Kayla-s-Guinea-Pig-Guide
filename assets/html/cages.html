C&C Cages

C&C cages are the most fitting enclosures for guinea pigs. They are customizable and allow for larger size cages than any pet stores contain. Most C&C cages can be made at home from choroplast and wired storage cubes. They can also be bought pre-made. One well known pre-made C&C is called the the Midwest cage.

Fleece Bedding

Fleece bedding is a better alternative to bedding such as Pine or Aspen. Fleece is reusable, no dust produced and easy to clean. Most fleece beddings contain an absorbant middle layer that wicks away urianation effectively. Many Esty's sellers make cage liners but you can also make them at home for relatively cheap.
