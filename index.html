About Me

My name is Kayla. I am an avid guinea pig owner. My goal is to educate people who may be thinking of owning a guinea pig. Guinea pigs are not as easy as they look. More often than not, their needs are neglected. I created this web page to help provide insight on the proper care for guinea pigs along with some common health issues you may encounter.
